About Mahatma Gandhi

Mahatma Gandhi, born Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi on October 2, 1869, was the leader of the Indian independence movement against British colonial rule...

Famous Quotes

"Be the change that you wish to see in the world."
"The best way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the service of others."
"An eye for an eye will only make the whole world blind."

Timeline of Major Events

  • 1869 - Born in Porbandar, Gujarat
  • 1888 - Traveled to London to study law
  • 1893 - Began activism in South Africa
  • 1915 - Returned to India
  • 1930 - Led the Salt March
  • 1942 - Launched Quit India Movement
  • 1947 - Witnessed Indian independence
  • 1948 - Assassinated in New Delhi
